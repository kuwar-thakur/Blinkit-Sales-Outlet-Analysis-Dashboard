#🚀 Project Report: Blinkit Sales & Outlet Analysis Dashboard

I recently designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Blinkit’s retail performance across outlets, items, and customer ratings.

🔑 Key Highlights:

$1.20M Total Sales across 10 outlets and 8,523 items

Top Performing Outlet Type: Supermarket Type1 (0.79M sales)

Outlet Location Insights: Tier 3 outlets lead with 472K sales

Item Analysis: Low Fat items contributed 0.73M sales, Regular items 0.41M

Top Categories: Fruits & Vegetables (178K sales), Snack Foods (175K sales), Household (136K sales)

Customer Engagement: Average rating of 3.92 across outlets

📊 Impact:  
This dashboard provides a clear view of sales distribution, outlet performance, and product category trends. It helps identify growth opportunities, optimize inventory, and improve customer satisfaction.

💡 Learning Outcome:  
Strengthened skills in data cleaning, modeling, DAX, and visualization while applying analytics to real-world retail scenarios.
